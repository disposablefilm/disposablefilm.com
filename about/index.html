What is the Disposable Film Festival?

Selected by Moviemaker Magazine as one of "The World’s Coolest Film Festivals," Disposable Film Festival has been, for a decade, a pioneer in bringing the best online video across the globe from the personal screen to the silver screen. Through screenings, workshops, competitions, panels, parties, and other events intended to educate and inspire, the DFF promotes and supports the next generation of filmmakers to enter and change the industry.

The program kicks off annually in San Francisco and is then seen in film festivals around the world throughout the year.
